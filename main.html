Kerala, India


Kerala is a stunning coastal paradise known for its lush greenery, serene backwaters, and rich cultural heritage. Whether you're looking for relaxation, adventure, or culinary delights, Kerala promises an unforgettable experience.

  1. Alleppey Backwaters – Experience the beauty of Kerala with a houseboat ride through the scenic backwaters.
  2. Munnar Tea Plantations – Walk through vast tea gardens and witness breathtaking hill station views.
  3. Kovalam Beach – A popular beach destination with golden sand and calm waters.
  4. Thekkady Wildlife Sanctuary – Explore rich biodiversity and spot majestic elephants in their natural habitat.
  5. Padmanabhaswamy Temple – One of the most famous and richest temples in India, known for its intricate architecture.
  • Appam with Stew – A delicious soft rice pancake served with a flavorful vegetable or meat stew.
  • Puttu – A unique steamed rice and coconut dish, often paired with kadala curry.
  • Kerala Sadya – A grand vegetarian feast served on a banana leaf, featuring multiple dishes.
  • Malabar Biryani – A fragrant, spiced biryani made with flavorful meat and aromatic rice.
  • Pazham Pori – Crispy banana fritters, a popular tea-time snack.
